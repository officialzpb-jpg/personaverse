Safety & Ethics

Our comprehensive approach to keeping PersonaVerse safe and ethical for everyone.

No Unauthorized Impersonation

We strictly prohibit creating AI personas of real individuals without their explicit written consent. Every creator persona is verified.

End-to-End Encryption

All conversations are encrypted in transit and at rest. Your data is protected with industry-standard AES-256 encryption.

Transparent Data Usage

We clearly explain how your data is used. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Secure Infrastructure

SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure with regular security audits and penetration testing.

Right of Publicity Compliance

We ensure all creator personas comply with right-of-publicity laws and have proper licensing agreements in place.

Content Moderation

AI-powered and human-reviewed moderation systems to prevent harmful content and misuse.

Privacy Policy Summary

At PersonaVerse, we take your privacy seriously. This summary explains how we handle your data:

Data Collection: We collect only what's necessary to provide our services - account info, chat history, and usage data.
Data Usage: Your data is used to improve our AI models and provide personalized experiences. We never sell your data.
Data Retention: Chat histories are retained for 90 days by default. You can delete your data at any time.
Your Rights: You can access, export, or delete your data at any time from your account settings.
